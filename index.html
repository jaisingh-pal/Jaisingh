Selected work

Explorations in silicon.

ICEE 2025 · CFET

Parasitic Capacitance in Tapered CFETs

3D TCAD and an analytical elliptical-integral model quantifying how process-induced channel taper raises parasitic capacitance — up to 23% for 3-sheet devices, with junction capacitance the dominant, most taper-sensitive contributor.

Read on IEEE Xplore ↗
IEEE T-ED 2025 · CFET

Analytical Model of CFET Parasitic Capacitance

A single-fit-parameter compact model for every parasitic component in CFETs via the elliptical integral method — capturing the ~20% separator-capacitance contribution and benchmarked against lateral nanosheet FETs.

Read on IEEE Xplore ↗
TCAD · GAA Nanowire

Design & DC Analysis of a GAA Si Nanowire nFET

2D r–z cylindrical Sentaurus simulation of a 6 nm gate-all-around Si nanowire (EOT 0.9 nm) with density-gradient quantum corrections and band-to-band tunnelling: ION/IOFF ≈ 7.5×10⁵, SS 63.5 mV/dec, DIBL 15.9 mV/V.
