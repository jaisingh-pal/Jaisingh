Research Projects

2D TCAD HBT Simulation
Advanced TCAD simulation of HBT structures incorporating heterojunctions between different semiconductor materials. Focus on high-frequency and high-speed applications with superior performance characteristics compared to traditional BJTs.
3D CFET Structure
Comprehensive study of CFETs integrating n-type and p-type transistors on single substrates. Enhanced power efficiency, reduced heat dissipation, and potential for 3D integration addressing miniaturization challenges.
Radiation-Hardened SRAM
Speed and power optimized design for space applications. Implementation of robust design methodologies, hardened materials, and redundancy techniques to enhance radiation tolerance of semiconductor devices.
Analog Circuit Design
Low-power 2-stage OP-AMP design in SCL 180nm technology. Band-gap reference circuit with temperature compensation and configurable amplifier analysis including CS-CS cascade, telescopic cascode, and gain-boosted configurations.
3D NAND Flash
Comprehensive review of vertical stacking architecture for higher density storage. Analysis of improved performance, enhanced endurance and reliability features, and future trends in 3D NAND technology.
SiGe HBT Research
In-depth study of SiGe HBT fabrication techniques, device physics, and performance metrics. Focus on high-frequency applications in communication systems and exploration of future research directions.
